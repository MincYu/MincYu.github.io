I lead the S4 Lab (**S**calable, **S**ustainable, and **S**table **S**ystems Lab) at CUHK-Shenzhen. Our research focuses on building modern cloud and AI systems that can scale effectively, use resources efficiently, and operate reliably in practice. We maintain long-term collaborations with leading industry partners, such as Huawei, Alibaba, and Ant Group.

**We are actively recruiting self-motivated PhD and Master students, research assistants, and undergraduate interns** who are passionate about computer systems research and have strong system building skills. Applicants with prior experience in system research, programming competitions, or open-source projects are especially welcome. 

If you are interested in joining our lab, please email me your CV, transcripts, and any other materials that can demonstrate your research capability and potential.
